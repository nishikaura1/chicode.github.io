Mission in Action

Real stories from the communities we've served, showing the power of technical literacy.

Economic Empowerment for Refugee Women

In **Iran** and the **Congo**, we hosted specialized workshops for refugee women. Beyond basic coding, we focused on practical entrepreneurship—helping them build and launch their own **e-commerce businesses** to gain financial independence.

E-Commerce Track

Foundational Literacy in India

Partnering with an orphanage in **India**, Chicode established a technical literacy program. We are teaching children the basics of hardware and software, ensuring the next generation has the skills to compete in a digital world.
